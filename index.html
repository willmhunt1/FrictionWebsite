You're not weak for losing hours to your phone. Billion-dollar teams engineered it to be exactly that hard to put down, and a little friction beats raw willpower every time.

What it does

A gentle nudge, not a lecture

Three small changes that quietly break the autopilot.

🌬️

Breathe before you scroll

Pick the apps you want to slow down. Open one and Friction Frog asks for a short breathing exercise first; you get a few minutes, then it locks again. Just enough friction to break the autopilot.

🎨

Grayscale, your way

Color is what makes the scroll hard to quit. Go full-phone grayscale with color exceptions for the apps you need (Photos, Camera, Maps), or keep color and gray out only the worst offenders. No more triple-tapping a shortcut you forget to switch back.

⏱️

Limits that fit you

Set your own time window per app. One app's "quick check" might be 1 minute, another's 30. You decide.
