Privacy Policy

For the frictionfrog.com website · Last updated June 2026

This page explains what happens when you visit frictionfrog.com. Short version: almost nothing. It's a plain website with no accounts, no forms, and no backend server collecting anything about you.

What this site collects

The site uses GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics tool. It does not set cookies, does not track you across other websites, and does not collect personal information. It records only aggregate, non-identifying visit statistics, such as:

  • which page was viewed
  • the referring site or marketing channel you arrived from (e.g. a link from Instagram or TikTok)
  • a rough, country-level location and your browser/device type

This helps me understand which pages are useful and which channels people find the app through. That's it. None of it is sold, shared, or used to identify you.

What this site does not do

  • No advertising or ad trackers.
  • No cookies, no fingerprinting, no cross-site tracking.
  • No accounts, sign-ups, or contact forms. The only way to reach me is the email link below, which you choose to use.

The app is separate

This policy covers the website only. The Friction Frog iOS app has its own privacy policy (shown on its App Store listing), which covers how the app itself handles data on your device.
