Support the project

Friction Frog is free, and it's staying that way. No ads, no paywall on the parts that help.

I'm one person building this in my spare time. The blocking and grayscale are free forever because the tools that help you use your phone less shouldn't be locked behind a subscription, and because tying my income to your screen time would be a little ironic.

If Friction Frog earned a spot on your phone and you want to chip in, support goes straight toward keeping it independent, ad-free, and improving. It covers the boring costs (developer fees, hosting) and the time to build new features.
